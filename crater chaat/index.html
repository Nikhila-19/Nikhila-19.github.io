Crater Chaat

Enjoy the authentic Martian taste with this exquisite bowl of deliciousness, namely the crater chaat.

Instead of the boring sev puri with tamarind sauce, taste the food of Mars to its best extent; a mixture of crunchiness, sweet and sour!

A superb option for lovers of Indian Cuisine...chaats ranging from aloo tikki, bhelpuri, ragda patties, bhalla and many more with complementary asteroid truffles are available!
