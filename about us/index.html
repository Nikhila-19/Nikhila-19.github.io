HOW WE WORK

We work simply as a fast food chain restaurant that provides you with both online delivery and a dine in sitting.We believe making food is too an art form and a reflection of our culture.

At our place, Chefs are not only people responsible for making this place what it is is!

Our designs and ideas are inspired from the past, which gives you a sense of familiarity.Trajectory Tea is inspired by the Ferris Wheel as is Propulsion Pulao inspired from Sizzling Sizzlers!

It is the output of the hardwork of artists and designers! This collaboration between diverse forms of art is what we cherish!
