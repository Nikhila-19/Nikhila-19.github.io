Supernova Sandwich - Explosion of Relish

As the name suggests, a great combination of healthy grean veggies...combined in such a manner that causes an explosion of relish and hence the name.

Available in many different shapes and forms, the open-faced, plain sandwich; pinwheels; closed tea sandwiches for the ones in a rush; ribbon sandwiches apt. for kids and mosaic sandwiches.

A wide variety of options for fillers too, cheese; ham and cheese, sausage; egg mayonnaise and custom combos accorfing to customer preferance.

Many combos available too, good deals at a reasonable price! Great option for school students, consisting of cookies, smoothies, crisps, salads and veggies!
